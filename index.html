The Moat

Lock-in tactics we fight

These are the patterns vendors use to make leaving painful. We've seen them all — and we know how to beat them.

🔒

Proprietary Data Formats

Your data stored in formats only their tools can read. Designed to make export a nightmare.

🕸️

Artificial Dependencies

Bundling features so removing one product means breaking five others. Complexity as a retention strategy.

💸

Punishing Exit Costs

Early termination fees, data extraction charges, and "migration assistance" priced to discourage leaving.

⏳

Slow-Drip Price Hikes

Small annual increases that compound until you're paying double — but switching feels too expensive to bother.
